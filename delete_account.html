Delete Your VoiceSum AI Account

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Option 1: Delete from inside the app (Recommended)

  1. Open the VoiceSum AI app
  2. Go to Settings (bottom right tab)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Result: Your account and all data (recordings, transcripts, folders) are deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion via email

If you no longer have access to the app (lost phone, uninstalled), email us at:

support@voicesum.ai

Include in your email:

  • The email address associated with your VoiceSum AI account
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

What data is deleted?

  • Your account (email, name)
  • All voice recordings
  • All transcripts and AI summaries
  • All folders and organization
  • Reminders and notifications
  • Premium subscription (auto-cancelled)

What data is retained?

  • Anonymous analytics (no personal info)
  • Payment records (required by law, but no personal data)

How long does deletion take?

Account and data deletion is processed within 30 days of your request. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.
